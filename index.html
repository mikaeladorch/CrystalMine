Artist Statement

This game's concept is inspired by finding crystals with my sister on hiking adventures. We would find various crystals hiding, and I thought it would be great to create that experience into a game.

In this game, the player is in a simulation of gathering crystals at a mining site.

Once opening the crystal mining simulation, players are introduced to a start screen that switches to an instruction screen.

Then, the player enters the mining field full of hidden crystals placed randomly. It's the player's job to collect all twenty crystals.
In the finale, the player receives a surprise at the end screen.
